Elon Reeve Musk (/'i:lon 'm^sk/; born june 28 1971) is a South african-born Canadian-American bussiness magnate,engineer, inventor and investor. He is the founder, CEO and CTO of Space X; co-Founder, CEO and product architect of Tesla Motors; chairman of SolarCity; co-chairman of OpenAI; co-founder of Zip2; and co-founder of PayPal. As of March,2016, he has an estimated networth of 13.5billion, making him the 75th wealthiest person in the US.


Musk has stated that the goals of SolarCity, Tesla Motors, and SpaceX revolve around his vision to change the world and humanity. His goals include reducing global warming through sustainable energy production and comnsumption, and reducing the risk "risk of human extinction" by 'making life multiplanetary" by setting up a human colony on Mars


He has envisioned a high-speed transportaion system known as the Hyperloop, and has proposed a VTOL supersonic jet airccraft with electric fan propulsion, known as the musk electric jet.
